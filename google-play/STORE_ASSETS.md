# Store Assets Requirements for Google Play

## Required Graphics and Screenshots

Google Play requires specific graphic assets for your app listing. This document outlines all requirements.

---

## 1. App Icon

### High-Resolution Icon
- **Size:** 512 x 512 pixels
- **Format:** PNG (32-bit)
- **Max File Size:** 1024 KB
- **Requirements:**
  - Full square, no rounded corners
  - No transparency
  - High quality, recognizable at small sizes

**Guidelines:**
- Use brand colors
- Include recognizable AI/art symbol
- Keep design simple and clear
- Test at various sizes (48dp, 96dp, 144dp)

---

## 2. Feature Graphic

### Main Feature Graphic
- **Size:** 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max File Size:** 1024 KB
- **Requirements:**
  - Landscape orientation
  - High quality
  - No text overlay recommended

**Usage:**
- Displayed prominently in Play Store
- Used in promotional materials
- May be cropped on different devices

**Design Tips:**
- Showcase AI art generation
- Use engaging visuals
- Highlight key features
- Brand consistent with icon

---

## 3. Phone Screenshots

### Requirements
- **Minimum:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum:** 8 screenshots
- **Size:** 
  - Minimum dimension: 320 pixels
  - Maximum dimension: 3840 pixels
  - Aspect ratio: 16:9 to 9:16
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max File Size:** 8 MB each

### Recommended Sizes
- **1080 x 1920 pixels** (9:16 - Portrait)
- **1920 x 1080 pixels** (16:9 - Landscape)

### Screenshot Content
1. **Home/Landing Screen** - Platform overview
2. **AI Generation Interface** - Creating art
3. **Gallery/Artwork Display** - Generated artwork examples
4. **Marketplace** - Buying/selling interface
5. **User Profile/Wallet** - Account features
6. **Token/Rewards System** - Gamification
7. **Settings/Privacy Controls** - User control
8. **Generated Art Examples** - Showcase quality

**Guidelines:**
- Show actual app interface
- Use real or realistic content
- Highlight key features
- No text overlays or borders (optional)
- High-quality, clear images
- Show app in best light

---

## 4. Tablet Screenshots (Optional but Recommended)

### 7-inch Tablet
- **Minimum:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum:** 8 screenshots
- **Recommended Size:** 1200 x 1920 pixels (or 1920 x 1200)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG

### 10-inch Tablet
- **Minimum:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum:** 8 screenshots
- **Recommended Size:** 1600 x 2560 pixels (or 2560 x 1600)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG

**Notes:**
- Show tablet-optimized interface if available
- Demonstrate responsive design
- Highlight multi-pane layouts

---

## 5. Promotional Graphics (Optional)

### Promo Graphic
- **Size:** 180 x 120 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Usage:** Featured in app promotions

### TV Banner (if TV app)
- **Size:** 1280 x 720 pixels
- **Format:** PNG
- **Usage:** Android TV home screen

### Wear OS Screenshots (if applicable)
- **Size:** 384 x 384 pixels minimum
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG

---

## 6. Promotional Video (Optional but Recommended)

### YouTube Video
- **Length:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes recommended
- **Format:** YouTube URL
- **Content:**
  - App demonstration
  - Key features showcase
  - User testimonials (if available)
  - Platform overview

**Tips:**
- Keep it concise and engaging
- Show actual app usage
- Highlight unique value proposition
- Include call-to-action
- Professional quality

### Before Submission
- [ ] All images at correct dimensions
- [ ] High-resolution, no pixelation
- [ ] Consistent branding across assets
- [ ] No copyrighted material (unless licensed)
- [ ] No misleading content
- [ ] Readable text (if any)
- [ ] Proper aspect ratios
- [ ] File sizes within limits
- [ ] Correct file formats

### Quality Standards
- [ ] Professional appearance
- [ ] Clear, crisp images
- [ ] Good color contrast
- [ ] No blurriness or artifacts
- [ ] Proper lighting in screenshots
- [ ] Representative of actual app

## Design Recommendations

### Color Palette
- Use brand colors consistently
- Ensure good contrast
- Consider accessibility (color blindness)
- Test on different screens

### Typography
- Readable fonts
- Appropriate sizes
- Clear hierarchy
- Avoid excessive text in images

### Composition
- Follow rule of thirds
- Clear focal points
- Balanced layouts
- Professional appearance

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Wrong dimensions or aspect ratios
❌ Low-resolution images
❌ Misleading screenshots
❌ Copyrighted content
❌ Excessive text in graphics
❌ Inconsistent branding
❌ Poor quality or blurry images
❌ Not showing actual app interface
❌ Screenshots from different apps

---

## Testing Before Submission

### Checklist
1. View assets on different device sizes
2. Check readability at small sizes
3. Verify colors appear correctly
4. Test on light and dark backgrounds
5. Review for typos or errors
6. Get feedback from others
7. Compare with successful apps
8. Ensure compliance with Google policies

---

## Google Play Policy Compliance

### Graphic Content Policy
- No sexual or suggestive imagery
- No graphic violence
- No misleading information
- No impersonation
- Age-appropriate for rating

### Asset Metadata Policy
- Accurate representation of app
- No deceptive practices
- No excessive claims
- Professional appearance
