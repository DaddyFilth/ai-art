# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 11, 2026**

### 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Art Revenue Exchange ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our Platform.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Personal Information
- Email address (hashed for privacy)
- Username and display name
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile information (avatar, bio)
- Age verification status
- Payment information (processed by Stripe)

#### 2.2 Usage Information

- Browser type

#### 2.3 Content Information

### 3. How We Use Your Information

3.1. **Service Provision**: To provide and maintain the Platform's functionality.

3.2. **Account Management**: To manage your account and authenticate your identity.

3.3. **Transactions**: To process payments and maintain transaction records.

3.4. **Security**: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

3.5. **Communication**: To send service-related notifications and updates.

3.6. **Analytics**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the Platform.

3.7. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

### 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

#### 4.1 With Your Consent
We share information when you explicitly consent, such as:
- Public profile information
- Marketplace listings
- Shared artwork

#### 4.2 Service Providers
We share information with:
- Stripe (payment processing)
- AWS (cloud storage)
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

#### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by:
- Court order
- Subpoena
- Law enforcement request
- Legal process

#### 4.4 Data Monetization (With Consent)
If you opt into data sharing:
- Prompt metadata may be aggregated and anonymized
- Usage patterns may be analyzed
- Datasets may be sold to third parties
- **No personally identifiable information is ever sold**

### 5. Data Security

5.1. We implement industry-standard security measures:
   - AES-256 encryption for data at rest
   - TLS 1.3 for data in transit
   - Secure authentication (bcrypt password hashing)
   - Regular security audits

5.2. Financial data is protected by:
   - Double-entry accounting
   - Immutable transaction logs
   - SHA-256 hashing for integrity verification

5.3. Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable.

### 6. Data Retention

6.1. We retain your information for:
   - Account information: As long as your account is active
   - Transaction records: 7 years (for legal compliance)
   - Usage logs: 90 days
   - Deleted content: 30 days (soft delete), then permanent deletion

6.2. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.
